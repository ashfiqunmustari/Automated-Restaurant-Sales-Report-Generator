**Local Restaurant Sales Dataset**

This dataset captures real world like sales transactions from a small local restaurant. It includes information about daily food and beverage orders, payment types, sales timing, and staff details.

**Dataset Overview**

Each record shows a single transaction with the following details:

order_id – Unique identifier for each order

date – Date of the transaction

item_name – Name of the food or beverage item ordered

item_type – Category of the item (Fast Food or Beverage)

item_price – Price per unit of the item

quantity – Quantity ordered by the customer

transaction_amount – Total amount paid

transaction_type – Payment method (Cash, Online, Others)

received_by – Gender of the staff handling the order

time_of_sale – Time of day (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night, Midnight)
